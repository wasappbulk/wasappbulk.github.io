Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

1. Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide our service:

  • Email address — for account creation and login
  • Password — stored as a secure hash, never in plain text
  • Usage statistics — message count and dates to enforce plan limits

We do NOT collect phone numbers you message, message content, your WhatsApp contacts, or any personally identifiable information about your recipients.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your account and manage your subscription plan
  • To track daily message usage against plan limits
  • We do NOT sell, share, or provide your data to third parties
  • We do NOT store your messages or contact lists on our servers

3. Data Storage

  • Your phone number lists and messages are processed locally in your browser only
  • Authentication tokens are stored in Chrome local storage on your device
  • Account data (email, usage stats) is stored in a private Supabase database
  • Supabase is GDPR-compliant and SOC 2 Type 2 certified
  • No message content or recipient phone numbers ever leave your browser

4. Your Rights

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us
  • Uninstall the extension to automatically remove all locally stored data
  • Request a copy of your data via email

5. Security

  • All communications use HTTPS encryption
  • WhatsApp's own end-to-end encryption protects your messages
  • No third-party tracking scripts or advertising cookies
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text

6. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for authentication and usage tracking. Supabase is GDPR-compliant. Your data is not shared with any advertising or analytics platforms.

7. Contact
