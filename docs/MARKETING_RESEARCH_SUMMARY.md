## 💡 Key Insights

### Market Opportunity
- **India = 40% of WhatsApp's global user base** (487M users)
- Small businesses spend **₹10K–20K/month** on digital marketing tools
- WhatsApp **98% open rate** vs 20% for email
- API costs in India are **75% lower** than global markets

### Competitive Advantage
- **Only genuine free tier** (SheetWA, WASender are paid-only)
- **No API needed** (vs complex WhatsApp Business API setup)
- **No credit card required** for free plan
- **Simpler UX** than ProSender, SheetWA

## 🎲 Competitor Marketing Analysis

### SheetWA
- **Strength:** Strong blog content (50+ posts), good SEO (DA 35+)
- **Weakness:** No free plan (₹749/month minimum), complex Google Sheets integration
- **Our Angle:** Genuine free tier, simpler CSV upload

### WASender
- **Strength:** Clean branding, smart anti-ban messaging
- **Weakness:** Paid-only (₹1,999/month), no free tier
- **Our Angle:** Free 10 msgs/day, cheaper Pro plan (₹1,599/month)

### ProSender
- **Strength:** 100K+ users, simple UI
- **Weakness:** Unclear free tier limits, complex UI for beginners
- **Our Angle:** Transparent limits, cleaner onboarding
